Research

Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing Lab

We develop novel computational algorithms and mathematical tools to address fundamental scientific and engineering problems. In particular, we consider Polygonal Staggered Discontinuous Galerkin methods, Polygonal mesh generation via machine learning, finite volume methods, adaptive FEM for elliptic and parabolic equations and their applications to fluid and solid mechanics.
